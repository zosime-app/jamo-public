## English

### 1. Overview

Jamo ("the App") is developed and operated by zosime. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

### 2. Information We Collect

#### 2.1 Automatically Collected Information
- **Anonymous User ID**: When you first open the App, a random anonymous ID is generated via Firebase Authentication and stored locally on your device. This ID is used to restore your session and save your learning progress. It does not contain your name, email, or any personally identifiable information.

#### 2.2 Information You Provide
- **Social Login (Google / Apple)**: If you choose to sign in with Google or Apple, we receive your name and email address from the respective provider. This information is used solely to identify your account.
- **Voice Input**: If you use the speech recognition feature, audio is processed locally on your device or by the operating system's speech recognition service (iOS Speech Framework / Android SpeechRecognizer). We do not store or transmit your voice recordings.

#### 2.3 Usage Data
- **Learning Progress**: Data about completed lessons or interactions within the App may be stored in Firebase Firestore to sync your progress across sessions.

### 3. How We Use Your Information

| Purpose | Data Used |
|---------|-----------|
| Session restoration | Anonymous User ID |
| Account identification (signed-in users) | Name, Email |
| Saving learning progress | Anonymous User ID, progress data |
| Audio playback | Firebase Storage (read-only) |

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or sale to third parties.

### 4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

| Service | Provider | Purpose | Privacy Policy |
|---------|----------|---------|----------------|
| Firebase Authentication | Google LLC | Anonymous auth, social login | [firebase.google.com/support/privacy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy) |
| Firebase Firestore | Google LLC | Learning progress storage | [firebase.google.com/support/privacy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy) |
| Firebase Storage | Google LLC | Audio file delivery | [firebase.google.com/support/privacy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy) |
| Google Sign-In | Google LLC | Social login | [policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) |
| Sign in with Apple | Apple Inc. | Social login | [apple.com/legal/privacy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy) |

### 5. Data Retention

- **Anonymous session data**: Retained until you uninstall the App or request deletion.
- **Account data (signed-in users)**: Retained until you delete your account or request deletion.
- You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting zosime1025@gmail.com.

### 6. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

### 7. Your Rights

Depending on your region, you may have the right to:
- Access the personal data we hold about you
- Request correction or deletion of your data
- Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact: **zosime1025@gmail.com**

### 8. Security

We use Firebase's security infrastructure (TLS encryption, access rules) to protect your data. Anonymous User IDs are stored locally using platform-secure storage (SharedPreferences / UserDefaults).

### 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the App or on our documentation page with an updated effective date.

### 10. Contact

**Email:** zosime1025@gmail.com

#### 2.1 자동 수집 정보
- **익명 사용자 ID**: 앱을 처음 실행하면 Firebase Authentication을 통해 무작위 익명 ID가 생성되어 기기에 저장됩니다. 이 ID는 세션 복원 및 학습 진도 저장에 사용되며, 이름·이메일 등 개인을 식별할 수 있는 정보를 포함하지 않습니다.

#### 2.2 이용자가 제공하는 정보

- **음성 입력**: 발음 연습 기능 사용 시, 음성은 기기 내 또는 운영체제의 음성 인식 서비스(iOS Speech Framework / Android SpeechRecognizer)에서 처리됩니다. 음성 데이터는 앱 서버로 전송하거나 저장하지 않습니다.

#### 2.3 이용 데이터
